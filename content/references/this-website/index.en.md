**Challenge.** A website that is its own proof: lightning-fast, accessible, free
of cookies and tracking, and entirely in our own hands.

**Solution.** A static site built with Hugo, self-hosted on our own server;
cookieless, log-based analytics, proof-of-work spam protection with no third
party, two languages, and automated build and deploy.

**Result.** ~24 KB CSS+JS, WCAG AA, zero tracking cookies, two languages — and
you're looking at it right now.
