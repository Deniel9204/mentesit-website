**Challenge.** Knowledge scattered across multiple, fragmented sources — the need
was searchable, reliable, cited answers.

**Solution.** We built an internal RAG system that ingests and indexes content
from multiple sources and returns cited answers — with a high-performance Rust
backend and a Svelte interface.

**Result.** Fast, accurate, cited answers over your own data, on your own
infrastructure.
