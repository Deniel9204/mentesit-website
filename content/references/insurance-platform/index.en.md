**Challenge.** Compare products from multiple insurers — mandatory motor, CASCO,
home and travel insurance — in one place, make them purchasable online, and
handle claims too, with high reliability and performance demands.

**Solution.** We designed and built the whole system: 20+ front-end and back-end
applications on Angular, Java, MongoDB and PostgreSQL — from architecture to
rollout.

**Result.** A single platform where users compare and buy insurance online, and
insurers reach the market in one place.
