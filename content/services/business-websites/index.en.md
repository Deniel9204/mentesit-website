A website shouldn't be hard work — not for your visitors, and not for you. We build fast, clean sites that load in a fraction of a second, rank well in search, and stay painless to update years down the line.

## What we build

Most of our website work falls into a few well-defined shapes:

- **Brochure sites** for small businesses: who you are, what you offer, references, contact — clear structure, real content, no filler.
- **Landing pages** for a specific campaign, product or service, built around a single clear goal.
- **Multilingual sites**, the way this very site runs in Hungarian and English.
- **Contact forms** with spam filtering and processing on a Hungarian server — not in a third party's hands.

If you later need an internal tool or a web app, the same team that built the site carries it forward.

## Why it's fast

A fast website isn't a trick — it's a technology choice. We generate static pages: no heavy CMS grinding away on every request, no scripts loaded for no reason. The payoff is cheaper hosting, better Core Web Vitals, and higher Google rankings. SEO isn't bolted on afterwards; it's baked into the structure — clean HTML, sensible headings, quick response times.

We build cookieless wherever we can. For most of our sites that means no annoying cookie banner, because we simply don't collect tracking data on your visitors. Less legal overhead, fewer clicks, more trust.

## How we work

We start with a short conversation: who the site is for, what it needs to do, what you already have (copy, logo, photos). Then we design the structure, build it, and review a staging version together. We hand over content editing so you can update the text yourself — or leave it to us, whichever suits you.

We don't vanish after launch either. Hosting, domain, email and backups can all come from us, from one place — so there aren't three vendors pointing fingers when something stalls.

## Why us

The page you're reading is our own reference — built with exactly the static, privacy-first technology we'd recommend to you.
