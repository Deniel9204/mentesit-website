You get advice from a team that builds and runs software itself, day in and day out — we know the technology from doing it, not from slides. Consulting here is hands-on work: we sit down with your actual systems, understand what they do, and tell you what's worth it and what isn't.

## What it's for

Call us when you're facing a decision and don't want to move blind. We help shape technology strategy and software architecture — whether it's a new system or rebuilding an old one. We review stack and vendor choices: what's justified, what's just fashion, and where you're overpaying. When the house is already on fire, we run a tightly scoped code, infrastructure or security audit and hand you a concrete, prioritized list of problems and risks.

## Typical situations

- **Inherited system.** You've got software someone else wrote that nobody dares touch. A system review shows how stable it is, what it costs to maintain, and whether it's worth extending or replacing.
- **Second opinion on a proposal.** A vendor is pitching a large build or cloud migration. We check, technically, whether the price and the direction are realistic.
- **Scaling or slowdown.** The system is growing, but performance is dropping and costs are running away. We find the real bottleneck — often not where it first appears.
- **Audit pressure.** A partner or regulation requires an independent security audit and a documented assessment of where things stand.

## How we work

Briefly: honestly. First we understand the business goal, then we talk technology. After the review you get a readable report — not eighty pages of generalities, but concrete findings, risks, and a realistic roadmap. We don't chain you to us: if you take the next step with another team or in-house, the documentation is still yours and still usable.

We're vendor-independent. We won't push a particular platform or cloud just because it's convenient for us. Where it makes sense, we work against lock-in and needless cloud spend, and favour data-sovereign solutions kept in the EU — where your data stays yours.

## Why us

Because we've built and operated real, production systems: a full online insurance-comparison platform (Angular/Java, Mongo and Postgres, 20+ apps), a private RAG knowledge base (Rust/Svelte), and mobile, web and backend claims-handling systems, for example in the insurance sector. We know where an architecture decision hurts two years later, because in several cases we lived with the consequences ourselves.

If you have a specific question, a suspicious quote, or a system you're not sure about — let's start with a short, no-pressure call, and from there we'll see whether a deeper review is even needed.
