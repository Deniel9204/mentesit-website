Custom software, for us, isn't a re-skinned template: we start from your existing processes and build exactly what's missing. It pays off most when off-the-shelf tools stop fitting — when they leave too much manual work behind, or refuse to talk to the rest of your stack.

## What we build

We build web apps and internal systems that carry a specific workflow end to end: customer and case management, quoting and billing logic, admin panels, role-based access. That includes **system integration** — the point where your webshop, accounting, CRM and inventory finally see the same data, in real time.

- **Web app development** — internal tools, customer portals, admin interfaces
- **API** design and integration with third-party services
- **Process automation** — replacing repetitive manual steps
- Extending systems you already run, not always starting from zero

## Typical use-cases

The most common starting point is a process held together by spreadsheets and email that no longer scales. Other times you need a bridge between two systems: syncing data over an **API** so nobody copies anything by hand. Sometimes automating a single internal report or approval loop delivers the biggest win — fewer errors, faster turnaround.

We're comfortable at larger scale too. We've worked on complex systems made of dozens of applications (e.g. insurance-sector platforms, an online comparison platform, motor-claims mobile and web systems), so we know how a system stays maintainable even as it grows many times over the years.

## How we work

We ship in small, working increments so you see progress early and can adjust decisions as you go. No hidden complexity: a transparent process, honest estimates, and code another developer can pick up after us. A good **internal system** is one that doesn't chain you to us forever — clean structure and documentation are how we guarantee that.

You don't need a finished spec to start. Tell us where today's process breaks down, and we'll work out the details together.

## Why us

We work privacy-first by default. Where it makes sense, your application and your data run on your own infrastructure, or on Hungarian infrastructure we operate with trusted local partners — not scattered across foreign cloud providers. That's an advantage during development too: **process automation** and integrations stay transparent, there's no vendor lock-in, and you always know where your data lives.

We don't promise magic or buzzwords — we promise solid, working software, with long-term maintenance and evolution.

If there's a process you're currently pushing along by hand, or two systems that finally need to connect, get in touch for a short call — we'll go through what's genuinely worth building, and what isn't.
