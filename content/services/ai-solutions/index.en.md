We don't add AI for its own sake — we add it to do one specific, repetitive task faster and more accurately. The models run on your own server or VPS, so your data never leaves for a third-party cloud. That's the difference from most SaaS-based AI tools, and it's exactly what keeps the solution GDPR-clean.

## What it actually means

For us, "artificial intelligence" isn't a chatbot in the corner of your website — it's a few well-defined building blocks:

- **RAG over your own documents** — searchable, cited answers grounded in your internal policies, contracts, and knowledge base. Every answer shows which document and section it came from, so it stays verifiable.
- **AI agents and agentic workflows** — automating multi-step tasks (gathering data, comparing it, preparing a form or ticket), with a human approval point wherever the stakes are real.
- **LLM integration into your existing systems** — we wire the language model's capabilities into the CRM, back-office, or ticketing tools you already use, rather than bolting on a separate interface.

## Typical use-cases

Most requests cluster around a few recurring patterns. A customer-support or internal Q&A assistant that works strictly from your own knowledge base. Classification and key-fact extraction from incoming documents (contracts, claims, invoices) — we've built exactly this kind of high-volume document processing for the insurance sector, for example. Structuring free-text data, preparing reports, and automating repetitive, rule-based steps where someone used to copy data by hand from one system to another.

## How we work

We start with a short assessment: where you lose the most time, and whether the result there is measurable. If a task can be solved with a simple rule, we'll tell you — we don't sell AI where it isn't needed. We begin with a small step that has a demonstrable payoff, measure accuracy, and only ship what genuinely works reliably. The system runs on your infrastructure, and the training and input data stay in your hands.
