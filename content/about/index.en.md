**FreeIT** is a Hungarian IT company whose main focus is custom software
development. We also build smaller websites, run systems and provide hosting —
so a single partner can cover your entire digital operation.

The name is a play on words: *free* of the IT headache — and, when needed, we
*free* you from it.
